What's it do?

Right now, Heartbeat Monitor does two things:

  1. changes color of admin bar to indicate heartbeat lub/dub
  2. sends time of lub/dub to browser's JavaScript console

provided as-is for your usage. be free to fork and change as suits your needs; please, just mention me (give credit) for the original code.
